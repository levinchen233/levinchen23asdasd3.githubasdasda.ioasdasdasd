Unpaid Childcare

Unpaid work fulfils many important functions that directly affect the well-being and quality of people's lives and covers a variety of activities such as voluntary work, domestic work, and caring for others. Unpaid work in the household and voluntary work in the community also make a substantial contribution to the national economy and to Australian society(from ABS).

The data collected on unpaid child care can assist in the planning of local facilities and services. The community can also support the carers in a more efficient way by using the data. From 2011 to 2016, it is clearly indicated from census data that provide by “Australian Bureau of Statistics” - The rate of people providing childcare is decreasing by 0.2%, not a large number. And people who lived closer downtown will have larger increasing rate on providing unpaid childcare.

The Plan

The factors that affect providing unpaid childcare can be various. For example, a family with one significant earner are more likely to provide unpaid childcare. And of both of the parents need to work for the family, usually they don’t provide unpaid childcare. Therefore unpaid child care can be analysed from household income, household type, employment and educational attendance. To help people care for their children, we decide to make a childcare centre in Sydney CBD. This is because the area has the most office building acrossing the Sydney, and it is far from the most residential area. Therefore, building a childcare centre there can help people who work around bring their children to this place and pick up their children when the day off.

The specific site for the childcare centre we chose is in the Royal Botanic Gardens. It is a place with wide open space, close to water, good air and nice sunshine. There is some distance from the busy streets so there is no need to worry about the traffic safety. Children can either spend their time in the centre or go outside to enjoy the natural gifts. For the actual design on the child care centre. The purpose is to create transparency between people inside and outside. Therefore It uses glasses as the main facades, and there are both indoor and outdoor resting places which set on the first floor and second floor respectively. On the second floor, it offers different types of books and internet service. The most impressive thing will be on the third floor. There is an open air garden with different kinds of flowers. As the centre is close to water, it is a prefect relaxing spot for kids.
